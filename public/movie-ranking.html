The best movies according to Aziz


My Top 3 movies


The Godfather triology

Francis Ford Coppola's The Godfather triology is a masterpiece of cinema. The first two films are often considered among the greatest movies ever made, and the third film, while not as highly regarded, still has its moments of brilliance. The story of the Corleone family is a gripping tale of power, loyalty, and betrayal that has captivated audiences for decades.


The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a powerful story of hope and friendship set against the backdrop of a maximum-security prison. The performances by Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman are outstanding, and the film's themes of resilience and redemption resonate with audiences on a deep level.


Inception

Inception is a mind-bending thriller that explores the nature of dreams and reality. Christopher Nolan's direction and the film's stunning visual effects create a captivating experience that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats. The performances by Leonardo DiCaprio and the ensemble cast are top-notch, making Inception a must-see for any movie lover.
